Our Story

Our founders, cybersecurity experts Mounira Remini and Satish Chitupolu, launched Cloud-fence in 2023. The inspiration for Cloud-fence came from witnessing countless businesses struggle to properly secure their cloud workloads.

Cloud-fence was built to allow companies to easily deploy all the security services they need and immediately secure and gain visibility across their multiple cloud infrastrucures.

Our Vision

At Cloud-fence it is our mission to build a future without cumbersome legacy approach to network security in the cloud.

We work hand-in-hand with our customers and partners to deliver holistic security that is purpose-built for today’s cloud-first, and securely enables any business, anywhere.
